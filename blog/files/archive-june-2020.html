How to: Sun Salutation A

07/06/20 20:05
Sun salutations (Surya Namaskar in Sanskrit) are a great short practice by themselves, and form the basis of many vinyasa flow yoga classes. Getting to know the basics of the movements involved is the perfect way to start your own home yoga practice. Try a few rounds of this sequence 3-5 times in the morning to help wake you up.
